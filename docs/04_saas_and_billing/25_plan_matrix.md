# Pricing Plan & Feature Matrix: Nomen

Nomen structures platform access into five distinct pricing tiers: Free, Starter, Pro, Business, and Enterprise.

---

## 1. Plan Limit Matrix

| Plan Name | Monthly Cost | Workspaces Limit | Projects Limit | Generations / Month |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free** | $0 | 1 | 2 | 50 |
| **Starter** | $19 | 3 | 10 | 500 |
| **Pro** | $49 | 10 | Unlimited | 2,500 |
| **Business** | $149 | 25 | Unlimited | 10,000 |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | Unlimited | Custom |

---

## 2. Feature Access Controls

Feature access is dynamically checked via feature flags stored in Plan configurations:

- **AI Providers Toggles**: Free users query Gemini/Ollama only. Pro and Business plans unlock Claude and GPT-4.
- **PDF Export**: Starter and above unlock report downloads.
- **API Access**: Restrict API Key generation to Pro, Business, and Enterprise tiers.
- **Custom Branding & White-Labeling**: Exclusively available on Business and Enterprise plans.
- **Priority Queue**: Enterprise queries bypass standard queues to compile suggestions faster.
